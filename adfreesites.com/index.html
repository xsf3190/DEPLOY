Background

If you have ever used popular website or blog building tools under a "free" plan, then you will know that you pay a price in the form of random adverts invading your site. If you find the ads obtrusive, you can upgrade to a paid plan. All of which is perfectly reasonable given the need to fund these free services.

However, many web-based services are now being provided free of charge. To build a website all you need are a hosting service to serve the content, a CMS (Content Management System) to manage the content and a database to track visits and monitor performance. Ideally, you also want your content served from a fast CDN network.
